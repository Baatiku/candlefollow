Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you see my trades or account balance?

No. The bot runs entirely on your own machine or your private cloud instance. We have zero access to your IQ Option account, credentials, or trading history.

What if I close my laptop?

Use the Railway cloud deployment for 24/7 trading. The bot runs on Railway's servers so your laptop never needs to stay on. Click "Deploy to Cloud" above.

Can I use a REAL account?

Yes. Switch between Practice and Real accounts from inside the dashboard at any time. We strongly recommend testing on Practice first.

What happens when my license expires?

The bot stops trading and shows a license renewal banner. Your data and settings are preserved. Renew via WhatsApp to resume immediately.

Is the trial really free?

Yes. Enter BESTA-FREE-TRIAL when prompted. No credit card. No payment of any kind. One trial per hardware ID.

Does it work on Mac or Linux?

The .exe is Windows only. Mac and Linux users can run the bot with Docker (5-minute setup — see Setup Guide) or use the Railway cloud deployment.
